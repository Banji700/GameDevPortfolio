About Me

Hi, I'm Banji Shittu, a Games Developer who primarily works with Unity and C#. I have been developing with Unity since secondary school, continuing through college and university, where I built a strong foundation in game development and software engineering.

During my career, I worked as a Unity Developer at MXReality, where I developed interactive learning and simulation experiences using Unity and C#. This role gave me valuable experience working on real-world projects and collaborating within a professional development environment.

From a young age, I was fascinated by how technology worked behind the scenes. Whether it was video games or everyday applications, I was always curious about the systems and logic that made them function. Growing up, I often found myself wondering how game characters were programmed, how multiplayer systems connected players across the world in real time, and how gameplay mechanics were designed and implemented. This curiosity naturally led me to pursue game development, which I studied throughout college and university using Unity and C#.

Over time, I wanted to expand my knowledge beyond game development and gain a deeper understanding of how modern applications and large-scale systems are built. This led me into full-stack software engineering, where I developed applications using ASP.NET Core, Angular, SQL Server, and Azure. My experience with C# through game development made this transition feel natural, allowing me to quickly adapt to new technologies and development practices.

Today, I combine my knowledge of both game development and software engineering to approach problems from multiple perspectives. Exploring different areas of development has not only strengthened my technical skills but has also made me a more versatile and effective game developer.
